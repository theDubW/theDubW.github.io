My Projects

Ball Craze:

Ball Craze is a fun iOS (and soon Android) game I developed from scratch using C++ and a library called cocos2d-x over the summer between Sophmore and Junior year. You can find it on the Appstore if you search "Ball Craze", or download it from here. Leave a good review!

Printcase: Stata command

Printcase is a command I developed for Stata, a "general-purpose statistical software package", widely used by statisticians globally. I co-wrote a paper detailing its necessity and application with University of Chicago professor Dr. Jenny Trinitapoli, which is currently under review by the Stata Journal.

Coronavirus Sumulations:

I spent Spring Break creating both visual and mathematical models to simulate the spread of COVID-19 and the best ways to mitigate it in populations worldwide.
